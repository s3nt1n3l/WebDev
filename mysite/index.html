Evaluation

Published on January 22, 2015

I think that my site has a good colour scheme, and general layout. It is also really easy to navigate.

I have used colours that compliment each other, for example the grey background softens the orange text.

However, I do not particularly like the style sheet for the Tutorial page and the second Stylesheet linked to the about me page. That stylesheet is a big 'in your face' with the colours and font types.

If I had more time, I would definately go back and completely renew that stylesheet into something more acceptable.

However for a first shot at learning HTML/CSS I think I have done fairly well and like the Website a lot.
